My Design Style

My design style emphasizes products that are highly practical and easy-to-use, while being sensitive to health and sustainability. I have a strong affinity for products that meet this criteria while also being simple and aesthetically pleasing.

OneWheel For Women

I redesigned the OneWheel to make it more desirable for a females. Through user interviews, I discovered that women's primary concerns are the safety, price, and maintenance of the OneWheel. Parallel Taste Tasting

I compared 4 different varieties of apples: McIntosh, Granny Smith, Gala, and Red Delicious. Using a 1-5 scale, I rated each apple on its texture, taste, color, and the cognitive emotions I associate with the type of apple. I then made a bracket to confirm my findings in a taste-off. Gala won both times! I hope to do another bracket with 4 other apples and compare the winners.

Products I Find Desirable


Longchamp purse

Longchamp purses are very versatile. They are both lightweight and durable, and they have long flexible that can fit over winter coats. The purses have a top zipper to keep valuables safe and from pick-pocketers or falling out. The shape is meant to be bulky on the bottom but slim on top, which makes it easy to hold and carry. Longchamps collapse when they are empty, unlike stiff bags which are still bulky and cannot pack in a suitcase). Nylon is easy to clean and it keeps my stuff dry when it’s raining. The purse has a little pocket on top for my keys, which are really the only small thing I carry around. Longchamps are way more durable than most bags and can hold heavy books without the straps breaking. It’s the perfect size to hold a computer. It’s not flashy, so you can dress it up or down.
