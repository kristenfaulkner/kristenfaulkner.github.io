Past Projects

Rent My Pet

Rent My Pet is a website that allows users to rent out their pets to people who are interested in taking care of a cat or dog for a day. The website was built using Ruby on Rails, Backbone.js, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, JQuery UI, and Google Maps API. Users can filter selections by animal type, location, color, gender, and age.
